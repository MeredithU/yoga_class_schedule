Teacher Training

Our community of yoga teachers and practitioners is a creative and inspiring environment in which to explore the concepts of yoga instruction and support the unfolding of your inner teacher. Our instructors are committed to growth and to sharing the transformative practice of yoga through their teaching.

200-Hour Teacher Training

Our mission is to create a supportive, inclusive, and non-competitive atmosphere in which yoga practitioners can learn and grow. The 200-hour Teacher Training courses serve this mission by offering the aspiring or continuing yoga teacher or serious student a sound foundation in the principals of yoga theory and practice within the container of the training staff and fellow participants.

We bridge the ancient wisdom of yoga asanas with a modern and thorough understanding of anatomy and physiology. Our curriculum provides Teacher Training participants with the basic tools to begin leading groups of all levels and to continue on the path of yoga with grace and humble confidence.

This accredited course, which qualifies participants for certification as a Registered Yoga Teacher (RYT) through Yoga Alliance is now offered in two formats:

Modular Training

Made up of two parts, or modules

Basic Training: One long weekend per month from October through March at our studios.

Advanced Training Retreat: A week-long residential retreat at Snowy Ridge in Whistler, BC, offered every April.

Summer Intensive

A 4-week immersion course

Training: This option packs the time-tested Teacher Training Curriculum into one powerful month.

Students: Our Summer Intensive is designed for students and others with the flexibility to dive into their studies.

300-Hour Teacher Training

This 300-hour Teacher Training program is designed for those aspiring to advance upon their 200-hour certification.

We're launching an exciting, inter-disciplinary advanced yoga teacher training. Join expert presenters as they share their insight and passion across a range of yoga subjects, including teaching skills, anatomy, yoga therapy, asana, philosophy, pranayama, and meditation.

Consistent practice, teaching and personalized feedback from core faculty gives you the individualized attention you need to cultivate your practice and presence.

Combined with the mentorship program, our 300-hour is crafted to combine in-depth skill building with the personalized support that you need to launch your teaching to the professional level. The methodology of the 300-hour teacher training unites a meticulous understanding of alignment with the power and flow of the breath.

We're committed to training bio-mechanically astute teachers who can support their love of yoga with a profound appreciation for functional movement. 300-hour students will build upon their 200-hour certification in order to integrate their skills, gain personalized feedback, and advance their teaching.
